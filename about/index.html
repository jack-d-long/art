About

I'm an engineering student at Cornell University exploring self-expression through a variety of photographic media. My personal work focuses on creation of unsettling images in familiar spaces, and how modern software and hardware tools can inspire and augment analog processes.

My analog images are captured on 35mm and medium-format film, as well as directly on black and white negative photopaper. I make silver gelatin prints through traditional optical enlargement.
